Target Audience

Business owners and leaders in Odessa who share a common goal of promoting success in their business.

Two personas

Persona 1: A young married couple is planning to start a nutritional drink and shake shop here in Odessa. They have already started to market their buisness through social media like Facebook and Instagram. They already know there will be a demand for their products.

Persona 2: A retired woman has always had a hope of opening an antique store in town. She hasn't had recent experience in running a business, but took classes in college. She has lived here her whole life so knows almost everyone. She's hoping that by already being familiar with the other business owners, it will be easy to work the chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

#1: The couple wants to know who the other owners are the the buisnesses in town.

#2: They also would like to know how previous businesses that were in the food industry have performed.

#3: The retired woman asks the chamber if her store could participate in the local ladie's night activity coming up where each business has offers or discounts.

#4: She would also like assistance in networking through social media, and wants to know if there is anyone to help her.

#5: Both would like to enroll and start preparing for the activities that will be happening at this summers fair, that will promote their businesses.
